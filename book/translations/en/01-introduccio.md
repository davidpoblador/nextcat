# Introduction

## Who we are

Xarter is a Catalan software agency dedicated to building quality digital solutions. We work with companies and organizations that need custom, robust, and maintainable software.

## Our mission

To provide software development services with a firm commitment to quality, transparency, and technological sustainability. We believe that good software is built with clear processes, open communication, and a solid technical foundation.

## Founding values

- **Technical excellence**: We apply best practices in every project.
- **Transparency**: The client always knows where we are and where we are heading.
- **Sustainability**: We build software that lasts, not quick fixes that generate technical debt.
- **Collaboration**: We work with the client, not for the client.
