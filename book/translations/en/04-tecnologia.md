# Technology Stack

## Selection principles

We choose technologies based on objective criteria:

- Platform maturity and stability
- Quality of documentation and community
- Performance and scalability
- Long-term maintainability

## Areas of expertise

### Backend

We build robust and scalable services with Python, Go, and Rust. We prioritize architectural simplicity and performance.

### Frontend

We develop modern and accessible user interfaces. We focus on user experience and loading performance.

### Infrastructure

Automated deployment, containers, and orchestration. Monitoring and observability as first-class citizens.

## Free software

We are advocates of free and open-source software. We actively contribute to community projects and release our own tools whenever possible.
