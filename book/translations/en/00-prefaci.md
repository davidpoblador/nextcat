# Preface

## About this document

*Xàrter* is a Catalan word that refers to charter flights. We chose this name because *charter* in English also means a founding document, and that is exactly what this document is: the founding charter of our software agency.

This document defines who we are, how we work, what services we offer, and what technologies we use. It serves as a reference for team members as well as for clients and collaborators who want to understand the way we work.
